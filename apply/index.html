Overview

Led by award-winning Psyop veteran of 12 years, EGG Founder & Director Pakorn Bupphavesa, EGG’s goal is to join forces with the next generation of artists and create captivating visuals that spotlight pressing social issues.

During this 12-week program, up to 8 candidates will learn how to work as a team to develop the design, story and overall production based on a real-world creative brief. With guidance and mentorship from some of the most senior Psyop artists, you will experience a full production first hand.

This is your chance to see Psyop from the inside; meet and learn from working professionals; and participate in a project that is trying to make the world a better place.

About Psyop

Psyop tells visually compelling stories and builds engaging worlds, using a combination of animation, design, illustration, 3D, 2D and live action production. For every project, Psyop takes a uniquely tailored and detailed approach to making the very best possible product.
